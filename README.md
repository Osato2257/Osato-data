# PaperPoint Sales Performance Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the sales performance of a stationery company using Microsoft Excel. The objective was to identify key revenue drivers, profitable product categories, top-performing regions, and leading sales representatives through data analysis and visualization.

## Dataset

The dataset contains sales transaction records with the following fields:

- Invoice ID
- Date
- Sales Representative
- Region
- Customer Name
- Customer Segment
- Product Category
- Product Name
- Quantity
- Unit Price
- Revenue
- Cost Price
- Profit

## Analyses Conducted

### 1. Customer Segment Analysis
Analyzed revenue and profit across customer segments to identify the most valuable customer group.

### 2. Product Category Analysis
Evaluated product categories based on revenue and profit performance.

### 3. Sales by Region Analysis
Compared regional performance to identify the strongest and weakest markets.

### 4. Sales Representative Performance Analysis
Assessed sales representatives based on revenue and profit generated.

## Key Findings

- The Government sector generated the highest revenue.
- Office Furniture & Accessories generated the highest revenue and profit.
- Ibadan was the highest-performing region in both revenue and profit.
- Amaka Nwosu was the top-performing sales representative based on revenue and profit generated.

## Dashboard Features

The dashboard includes:

- Total Revenue KPI
- Total Profit KPI
- Top Customer Segment KPI
- Top Region KPI
- Customer Segment Performance Chart
- Product Category Performance Chart
- Regional Sales Performance Chart
- Sales Representative Performance Chart

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Dashboard Design

## Project Outcome

This project demonstrates the use of Excel for business performance analysis and dashboard creation, transforming raw sales data into actionable business insights.
